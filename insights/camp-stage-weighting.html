How Stage Weighting Changes CAMP Decisions

The same startup can look investable or risky depending on stage context. Here is how to frame your metrics so they match investor weighting logic.

Claim Type: Fact

Investors do not weight Capital, Advantage, Market, and People equally at every stage. Early rounds tend to tolerate more market uncertainty if team execution and learning velocity are high.

Claim Type: Estimate

For most seed-stage reviews, we estimate that execution discipline and clear milestone plans matter more than polished narrative quality during first-pass filtering.

Claim Type: Opinion

Founders lose credibility when they present a single static score without showing how score interpretation changes by stage risk profile.

What founders should do next

Build two versions of your readiness narrative: one for your current round and one for the next round. Show what will have to be true in 6-12 months to justify the next financing step.
