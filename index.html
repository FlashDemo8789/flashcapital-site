FAQ

Common questions

How does FLASH work?
FLASH uses the CAMP framework to evaluate your startup across four critical dimensions: Capital, Advantage, Market, and People. We analyze 45 key metrics that investors care about most, then provide you with a fundability score and specific recommendations for improvement.
Who built FLASH and why?
FLASH was built by founders who've experienced the fundraising struggle firsthand. After going through countless rejections and finally raising capital, we realized founders need honest feedback before they pitch. We created FLASH to democratize access to the insights that typically only come from expensive advisors or painful rejections.
Do I need a pitch deck to use FLASH?
No! That's the beauty of FLASH. Our guided questionnaire helps you share the right information about your startup without needing a polished deck. In fact, this can be a great way to understand what should go in your pitch deck when you do create one.
What's the catch?
No catch. We use AI to make faster, better decisions. That's our edge. While other VCs spend 6 months in diligence, we can decide in 2 weeks because our models have already analyzed what matters.
What if I score below 75?
You get specific feedback on what to improve. Not vague advice like "get more traction" but specific metrics: "Reduce CAC from $150 to $50" or "Increase retention from 60% to 85%." Fix it and reapply.
Do you take equity?
Yes, standard SAFE with a cap. Founder-friendly terms. We're not trying to own half your company. We want you focused on building, not negotiating terms for months.
How are you different from other VCs?
We decide based on data, not degrees. No Stanford bias. No "I need to see more traction" without defining what that means. Just clear metrics and fast decisions. Plus, you can reapply when you improve.
What companies have you funded?
We're selective - funding 1 in 20 applicants. B2B SaaS, developer tools, AI applications, marketplaces. If you're building real technology solving real problems, we're interested.
