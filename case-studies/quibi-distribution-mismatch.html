Quibi: Product-Distribution Mismatch

Quibi highlights the risk of strong production execution paired with weak audience-channel fit at launch.

Claim Type: Fact

Public reporting and executive commentary described adoption challenges tied to market behavior assumptions and distribution context at launch.

Claim Type: Estimate

When behavior assumptions are unproven, high upfront content spend can increase downside exposure before product-market evidence is established.

Claim Type: Opinion

Launch quality cannot compensate for unresolved behavior-channel mismatch.

What founders should do next

Before scaling spend, test your core consumption behavior in realistic channel conditions. Treat distribution fit as a first-order product decision.
