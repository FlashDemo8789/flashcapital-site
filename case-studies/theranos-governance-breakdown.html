Theranos: Governance and Verification Breakdown

This case is a reminder that weak claim verification and weak governance can destroy both company value and stakeholder trust.

Claim Type: Fact

Public legal outcomes and investigations established major gaps between external claims and verifiable operating reality.

Claim Type: Estimate

When technical claims are not independently verifiable, downstream financing risk usually rises sharply regardless of narrative strength.

Claim Type: Opinion

Verification discipline is not a compliance checkbox; it is a core trust asset.

What founders should do next

Create a claims registry for investor and customer statements. Assign an owner and evidence source for every major technical or performance claim.
