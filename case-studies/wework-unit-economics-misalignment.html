WeWork: Unit Economics Misalignment

This case highlights how top-line growth narratives can collapse when underlying margin structure and governance confidence diverge.

Claim Type: Fact

Public filings and market reporting documented governance concerns and business-model scrutiny around long-term lease liabilities versus revenue durability.

Claim Type: Estimate

When fixed obligations scale faster than predictable cash generation, financing optionality typically compresses rapidly in risk-off markets.

Claim Type: Opinion

Narrative intensity cannot substitute for structural margin coherence.

What founders should do next

Document your unit economics with downside scenarios before fundraising. Show where margins hold under slower growth assumptions.
